<Start: SRS-163769-HeNBFM-400, Requirement>
HeNB shall support buffering and sending all “unsent” notifications with the next periodic inform.
The option shall be configurable by the operator. (MIM Parameter is eNB:FMProfile::enablePassiveReporting)
NOTE: This shall not be supported in SCLR1.1 and shall be considered for SCLR1.1.0.
FRS Requirement: future
	Affected DPM Area: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
	Release: SCLR1.1          
	Network Element: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test
