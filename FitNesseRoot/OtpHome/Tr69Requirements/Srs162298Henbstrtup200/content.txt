<Start: SRS-162298-HeNBStrtUp-200, Requirement>
If the HeNB is provisioned with an initial Software image it shall use this to establish an IPsec tunnel to the default SeGW and register with the Initial HDM for further provisioning, as defined by the requirements in sections 4.3.4 & 4.3.5.
Otherwise the HeNB shall exercise its recovery software as defined in section 4.3.3.
Note: The HeNB bootloader can check in NAND for the presence of a factory provisioned software image.
