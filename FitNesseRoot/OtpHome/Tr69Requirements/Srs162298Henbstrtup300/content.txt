<Start: SRS-162298-HeNBStrtUp-300, Requirement>
On startup the HeNB shall check whether a software image has been provisioned on the HeNB. If no image is provisioned, the HeNB shall execute its recovery software.
Note: In order to do this the HeNB bootloader will check whether an image is loaded in NAND.
