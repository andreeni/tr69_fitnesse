<Start: SRS-163652-HeNBCM-700, Requirement>
HeNB shall support a GetParameterValuesResponse to a request for getParameterValues to return the configuration of the HeNB. The request shall contain the ParameterList (array of name-value pairs).
The fault codes returned are defined in Section A.3.2.2 [S 9]
