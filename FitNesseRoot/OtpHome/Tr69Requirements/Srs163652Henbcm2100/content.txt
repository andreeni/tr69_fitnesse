<Start: SRS-163652-HeNBCM-2100, Requirement>
For any configuration changes made on the HeNB (autonomously/via CLI) that is not originated from the northbound interface of the HeNB (i.e. via HDM or SCMS) shall generate a AVCN, ODN, OCN or BCN as appropriate. The notifications shall be sent as informs to the SCMS. 
Note: The behaviour of what kind of notification is generated will align with the behaviour defined in the eNB (macro) with feature 115149.
Detailed requirements on the format of the notification are defined in section 4.5 and feature 163769 requirements.
