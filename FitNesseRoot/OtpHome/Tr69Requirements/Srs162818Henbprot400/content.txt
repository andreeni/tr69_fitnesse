<Start: SRS-162818-HeNBProt-400, Requirement>
A TR-069 session shall always be initiated by the HeNB, based on the following conditions:
-	At factory start-up, On power-up or Reset
-	Once every Periodic Inform
-	Whenever a Parameter is modified that is required to initiate an Inform on change 
-	Whenever a download or upload completes 
-	When receiving the TR-069 Connection Request on a specific URL
-	HeNB shall support the TR-069 connection request mechanism to allow the management system to demand an immediate TR-069 session
-	Location Update
FRS Requirement: FRS-162818-Req-1          
	Affected DPM Area: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
	Release: SCLR1.1          

<End: SRS-162818-HeNBProt-400>
