<Start: SRS-HeNB-OAM-DeviceMgmt-200, Requirement>
The following TR-106 parameter shall be used to construct the DeviceID sent along each TR-069 Inform() message. The structure is used to uniquely identify the HeNB.

DeviceIdStruct definition
Name	Description	TR-106 Parameter 
Manufacturer	Manufacturer of the device (for display only).	Device.DeviceInfo.Manufacturer
OUI	Organizationally unique identifier of the device manufacturer. 	Device.DeviceInfo.ManufacturerOUI
ProductClass	Identifier of the class of product for which the serial number applies. 	Device.DeviceInfo.ProductClass
SerialNumber	Identifier of the particular device that is unique for the indicated class of product and manufacturer.	Device.DeviceInfo.SerialNumber 

FRS Requirement: FRS-162818-Req-7
Affected DPM Area: eNB-OAM, SCMS, Test 
Release: SCLR1.1
