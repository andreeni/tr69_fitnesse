<Start: SRS-162298-HeNBStrtUp-40, Requirement>
When feature 162298 is enabled the HeNB & HDM shall follow the defined startup procedure when triggered by any of the following conditions:
•	On first installation
•	When a factory reset is performed by the user pressing and holding the Factory Reset button on the HeNB 
•	When a factory reset is triggered by the HDM sending a TR-069 FactoryReset Method to the HDM.
•	When the HeNB is power cycled, triggered by its own software, e.g. when installing a new Bulk CM file
•	When HDM issues a TR-069 Reboot RPC Method to the HeNB
FRS Requirement: FRS-162298-Req-R16          
	Affected DPM Area: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
	Release: SCLR1.1          
	Network Element: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
<End: SRS-162298-HeNBStrtUp-40>
