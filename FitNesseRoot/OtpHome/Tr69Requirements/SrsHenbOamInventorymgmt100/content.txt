<Start: SRS-HeNB-OAM-InventoryMgmt-100, Requirement>
The inventory information shall be populated via the TR-106 parameters defined under DeviceInfo MO. They are, 
•	DeviceInfo::Manufacturer
•	DeviceInfo::ManufacturerOUI 
•	DeviceInfo::ProductClass
•	DeviceInfo::ModelName
•	DeviceInfo::SerialNumber 
•	DeviceInfo::HardwareVersion
•	DeviceInfo::SoftwareVersion
•	DeviceInfo::AdditionalSoftwareVersion
•	DeviceInfo::AdditionalHardwareVersion
	
The LMS shall be able to use TR-069 Get to retrieve these parameters. The inventory information is not available via bulkcm interface.  

FRS Requirement: FRS-162818-Req-1, FRS-162818-Req-7
Affected DPM Area: eNB-OAM, SCMS, Test 
Release: SCLR1.1          
Network Element: eNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
<End: SRS-HeNB-OAM-InventoryMgmt-100>
