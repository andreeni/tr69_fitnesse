<Start: SRS-163652-HeNBCM-1600, Requirement>
The HeNB shall have the capability to fallback to the previous configuration if the new configuration fails for at least the following reasons:
•	If, the configuration fails to validate on the HeNB
•	If, after a bulk CM update and reset the HeNB fails to connect to the OAM system then the HeNB shall automatically fallback to the previous configuration. 
•	If, during a SW upgrade there is a request from the SCMS to fallback, or if the SW fails during ugrade.

	Affected DPM Area: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
	Release: SCLR1.1
