<Start: SRS-163769-HeNBFM-800, Requirement>
HeNB shall support sending state change notifications (SCN) based on the eNB:: FMProfile::enableRealTimeSCN  settings configurable by the operator.
If enableRealTimeSCN is true:
•	Real Time: All SCNs are sent in real time.
•	Logged: SCNs are stored in the HENB and pulled only on request.
FRS Requirement: FRS-163769-R3          
	Affected DPM Area: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
	Release: SCLR1.1          
	Network Element: HeNB-OAM, SCMS, Test          
<End: SRS-163769-HeNBFM-800>
