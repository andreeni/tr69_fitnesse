<Start: SRS-162818-HeNBProt-450, Requirement>
HeNB and SCMS shall provide a configurable timer of Periodic Informs. The timer range shall include 0 (turn off periodic informs), 5 mins to 24 hours with increments in mins. Default: 8 hours.
Note: typical deployments shall be set to 8 hours, however for certain specific debugging or certain customers there may be a need for more proactive management.  The dimensioning guidelines need to clearly outline the consequences of reducing the intervals.
