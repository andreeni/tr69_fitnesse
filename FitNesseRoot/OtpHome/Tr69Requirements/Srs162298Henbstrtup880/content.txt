<Start: SRS-162298-HeNBStrtUp-880, Requirement>
If the registration timer expires the HeNB shall take the following actions: 
-  if no download is in progress, the HeNB shall restart the whole registration procedure (set registrationStatus=false and send Inform(BOOTSTRAP) to the Initial HDM).
-  if a download is in progress, the registrationTimer shall be restarted.
